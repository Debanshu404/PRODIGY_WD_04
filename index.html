My Introduction

I am a second-year B.Tech student in Computer Science Engineering, currently in my third semester. My passion for technology started with an interest in the visual and interactive elements of websites, leading me to specialize in front-end web development. My projects have not only enhanced my technical abilities but also my understanding of user experience and design principles. As I progress in my B.Tech studies, I am enthusiastic about the future and dedicated to making the most of every opportunity to advance my skills and knowledge. I am confident that with perseverance and dedication, I will successfully transition into a full-stack developer and contribute to creating innovative web solutions
